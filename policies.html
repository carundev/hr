Company Policies

  • Code of Conduct
    Upholds the standards of behavior expected from all employees to foster a respectful, inclusive, and productive workplace.
    • Treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism.
    • Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or bullying.
    • Maintain confidentiality of company and client information.
    • Report any unethical or inappropriate behavior to HR.
  • Leave and Attendance Policy
    Outlines the procedures and expectations for employee attendance, punctuality, and leave requests.
    • Employees must notify their manager and HR in advance for planned absences.
    • Sick leave, vacation, and other leave types must be recorded in the HR system.
    • Excessive absenteeism or tardiness may result in disciplinary action.
    • Refer to the employee handbook for leave entitlements and procedures.
  • Equal Opportunity Policy
    Ensures fair treatment and equal access to opportunities for all employees, regardless of background.
    • The company is committed to a diverse and inclusive workplace.
    • Employment decisions are based on merit, qualifications, and business needs.
    • Discrimination or retaliation based on race, gender, age, religion, disability, or any protected status is strictly prohibited.
    • Employees are encouraged to report any concerns to HR.
  • Remote Work Policy
    Defines the guidelines and expectations for employees working remotely.
    • Employees must be available during core business hours and attend all required meetings.
    • Maintain a safe and productive work environment at home.
    • Company equipment and data must be used responsibly and securely.
    • Regular check-ins with managers are required to ensure alignment and support.
  • Health and Safety Guidelines
    Promotes a safe and healthy work environment for all employees.
    • Follow all safety protocols and report hazards immediately.
    • Participate in mandatory health and safety training sessions.
    • Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
    • In case of emergency, follow the company's evacuation and response procedures.
